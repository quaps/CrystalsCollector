CRYSTALS COLLECTOR


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are 3 gems below. By clicking on a gem you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. If you exceed the random number, you lose.

The value of each gem is hidden until you click on it, & each time the game starts the value of the gems will change.
